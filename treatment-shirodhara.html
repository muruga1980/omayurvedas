About Shirodhara

Shirodhara is a profoundly calming Ayurvedic therapy where a continuous stream of warm, medicated oil is gently poured over the forehead, specifically targeting the third eye area. This ancient healing practice induces a deep state of relaxation, balances the nervous system, and harmonizes the mind-body connection.

How It Works

The warm oil stream creates a soothing vibration on the forehead, stimulating the pineal gland and activating the parasympathetic nervous system. This triggers the body's natural healing response, releasing accumulated tension and promoting mental clarity.
