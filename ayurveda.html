The Three Doshas

Understanding Your Constitution

Ayurveda recognizes three fundamental energies that govern our physical and mental processes.

air

Vata

The energy of movement — composed of space and air. Governs breathing, blinking, muscle movement, and heartbeat. When balanced, Vata promotes creativity and flexibility.

local_fire_department

Pitta

The energy of digestion and metabolism — composed of fire and water. Governs digestion, absorption, and body temperature. Balanced Pitta promotes understanding and intelligence.

water_drop

Kapha

The energy of structure and lubrication — composed of earth and water. Governs growth, immune system, and fluid balance. Balanced Kapha promotes love, calmness, and forgiveness.
