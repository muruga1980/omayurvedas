Who We Are

Our Founders

Meet the Healers

Guided by Vedic wisdom and years of dedicated practice in traditional Ayurveda.

Satchidananda Saraswati

Founder & Lead Practitioner

Born and raised in Tamil Nadu, Satchidananda moved to Rishikesh in 2003 and joined Swami Dayananda Ashram, Arsha Vidya Gurukulam, completing an intensive three-year residential course in Vedanta. His deeper study of the Atharva Veda opened the path toward Ayurveda, integrating Vedic mantra chanting with natural healing and holistic health.

Shrividya

Co-Founder & Healer

Born in Tamil Nadu, Shrividya worked in skill development for rural communities before dedicating herself to the study of medicinal herbs, cleansing methods, and natural healing practices. Under the guidance of Satchidananda Saraswati, she studied the Atharva Veda and traditional Ayurvedic practices, playing a vital role in patient care and practical guidance.
